Chocolate Chips Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 21/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup wasalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Prebeat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. In a small bowl, mix flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the flour mixhare until just blended.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a wooden spoon.
  6. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the edges are nicely browned.
  8. Cool on wire racks and enjoy!
