Holi is a two-day holiday. The day before the full moon is Holika Dahan. On this day, a bonfire is lit to burn away all bad and evil. The next day, after the full moon, is the festival of colors..

Upcoming Events

ORN Delhi, a vibrant educational institution, annually celebrates the festival of Holi with unparalleled enthusiasm. The campus transforms into a kaleidoscope of colors, echoing the joyous spirit of this ancient Hindu festival. Students and faculty come together to partake in the festivities, engaging in traditional rituals, vibrant color play, and cultural performances. Holi at ORN Delhi fosters a sense of unity, transcending cultural boundaries, and creating lasting memories for everyone involved. It is a time when the diverse student body comes together to celebrate the triumph of good over evil and the arrival of spring in a riot of colors.!
